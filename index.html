How to Use Vu Attendance

  • Dashboard: View overall attendance statistics and a graphical overview across all your classes.
  • Attendance: Create new classes, enroll students, and manage daily attendance sessions.
  • Reports: Download comprehensive PDF or Excel reports for entire classes or specific individuals.
  • Filter Defaults: Easily find students whose attendance falls below your required threshold.
